AKAN NAME GENERATOR

The Akan people are a meta-ethnicity living primarily in the countries of the present day Ghana and Ivory coast. The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born. For each day of the week, there is a name available for each gender

This web application helps you know the day of the week you were born and your Akan name based on you birthday and gender.
